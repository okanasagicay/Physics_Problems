# Task 03 – Biot–Savart Law for a Current Segment

## Problem Statement

A current segment of length $0.1 \, \text{m}$ carries $3 \, \text{A}$. It is $0.2 \, \text{m}$ from point $P$ and perpendicular to the line connecting them.

## Theory

Biot–Savart Law for a small segment:

$$
dB = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{I \, dl \sin\theta}{r^2}
$$

For $\theta = 90^\circ$, $\sin\theta = 1$.

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
B = \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7}}{4\pi} \frac{3 \times 0.1}{(0.2)^2}
$$

$$
B = 10^{-7} \frac{0.3}{0.04}
$$

$$
B = 7.5 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{T}
$$

## Final Result

$$
B = 7.5 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{T}
$$

## Interpretation

Magnetic field decreases with the square of the distance from the current element.
