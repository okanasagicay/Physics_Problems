# Task 07 – Wavelength and Frequency of Visible Light

## Problem Statement

Wavelength $\lambda = 550 \, \text{nm}$.

## Theory

$$
f = \frac{c}{\lambda}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\lambda = 550 \times 10^{-9} \, \text{m}
$$

$$
f = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{550 \times 10^{-9}} = 5.45 \times 10^{14} \, \text{Hz}
$$

## Final Result

$$
f = 5.45 \times 10^{14} \, \text{Hz}
$$

## Interpretation

This wavelength corresponds to green light.
