# Task 10 – Speed of Light in Diamond

## Problem Statement

Index $n = 2.42$.

## Theory

$$
v = \frac{c}{n}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
v = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{2.42} = 1.24 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}
$$

## Final Result

$$
v = 1.24 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}
$$

## Interpretation

Higher refractive index significantly reduces light speed.
