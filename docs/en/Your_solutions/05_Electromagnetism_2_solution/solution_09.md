# Task 09 – Refraction Using Snell's Law

## Problem Statement

Light goes from air ($n_1=1.00$) to glass ($n_2=1.50$) at $30^\circ$.

## Theory

$$
n_1 \sin\theta_1 = n_2 \sin\theta_2
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\sin\theta_2 = \frac{1}{1.5} \sin 30^\circ = 0.333
$$

$$
\theta_2 = 19.47^\circ
$$

## Final Result

$$
\theta_2 \approx 19.5^\circ
$$

## Interpretation

Light bends toward the normal in denser media.
