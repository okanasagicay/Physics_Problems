# Task 06 – Electromagnetic Wave Analysis

## Problem Statement

$E_y(x,t) = 100 \sin(10^7 x - \omega t)$.

## Theory

Wave form:

$$
E = E_0 \sin(kx - \omega t)
$$

where $k = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}$ and $\omega = c k$.

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
k = 10^7
$$

$$
\lambda = \frac{2\pi}{10^7} = 6.28 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{m}
$$

$$
\omega = c k = (3 \times 10^8)(10^7) = 3 \times 10^{15} \, \text{rad/s}
$$

Propagation is in $+x$ direction.

Magnetic field:

$$
B_z = \frac{E_0}{c} \sin(10^7 x - \omega t)
$$

## Final Result

Values as above.

## Interpretation

Electric and magnetic fields are perpendicular and propagate in the $+x$ direction.
