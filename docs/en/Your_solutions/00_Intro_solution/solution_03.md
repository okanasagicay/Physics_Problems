TASK 03 – PROPORTIONALITY (Universal Gravitation)

Problem:
F = G·(m₁·m₂)/r²
How does F change if r is doubled and both masses are halved?

Solution:

Initial: F = G·(m₁·m₂)/r²

New values:
m₁' = m₁/2
m₂' = m₂/2
r' = 2r

New force:
F' = G·(m₁'·m₂')/(r')²
    = G·((m₁/2)(m₂/2))/(2r)²
    = G·(m₁·m₂/4)/(4r²)
    = G·(m₁·m₂)/(16r²)
    = (1/16)·F

Final answer:
The force becomes 1/16 of its original value.
