TASK 05 – TRIGONOMETRY

Problem:
Vector A has magnitude 15 and angle θ = 60° with horizontal.
Find horizontal and vertical components.

Solution:

Horizontal component:
Aₓ = A·cos θ = 15·cos(60°)
cos(60°) = 1/2
Aₓ = 15·(1/2) = 7.5

Vertical component:
Aᵧ = A·sin θ = 15·sin(60°)
sin(60°) = √3/2
Aᵧ = 15·(√3/2) = (15√3)/2 ≈ 12.99

Final answer:
Aₓ = 7.5
Aᵧ = (15√3)/2
