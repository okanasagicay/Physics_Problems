TASK 07 – LOGIC & SERIES (Fly Problem)

Problem:
Bicycle 10 m from wall, moves toward wall at 1 m/s.
Fly starts from bicycle, flies to wall at 2 m/s, turns instantly, repeats.
Total distance fly travels before being crushed?

Solution:

Key insight: Time is the same for both.

Time for bicycle to reach wall:
t = distance/speed = 10 m / 1 m/s = 10 seconds

Fly flies continuously for 10 seconds at 2 m/s:
distance = speed × time = 2 m/s × 10 s = 20 m

Final answer:
The fly travels 20 meters.
