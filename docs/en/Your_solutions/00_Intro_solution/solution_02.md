TASK 02 – SYSTEMS OF EQUATIONS

Problem:
Solve: 2x + 3y = 12 and x - y = 1

Solution:

From x - y = 1:
x = y + 1

Substitute into 2x + 3y = 12:
2(y + 1) + 3y = 12
2y + 2 + 3y = 12
5y = 10
y = 2

Then x = y + 1 = 3

Check:
2(3) + 3(2) = 6 + 6 = 12 ✓
3 - 2 = 1 ✓

Final answer:
x = 3, y = 2
